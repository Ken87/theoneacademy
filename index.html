OUR THEME

Naked 1308 is a collective of 23 young Multimedia Designers who are highly skilled and passionate in their expertise. In conjunction with this exhibition, we invite you to be part of our celebration!

" Naked " is a term of being fresh, clean and pure. Using bathroom as a metaphor, we resonate ourselves to an experience of a nice bath, being "fresh and polished" after three years under the running showerhead of The One Academy, and ready to "get dressed" to face the real world.
